Achievement Networking
Achievement Description Reach a 5-heart friend level with 10 people.
Purchase Price 2,000g
Sell Price Cannot be sold
The Santa Hat is a hat that can be purchased from the Abandoned House for 2,000g after earning the "Networking" Achievement (reach a 5-heart friend level with 10 people). It can also be obtained from Emily's outfit services at the Desert Festival. There is a ≈2% chance[1] to receive the gender-neutral outfit with the Santa Hat.
Notes
The Santa Hat outfit also includes the Fancy Red Blouse and Shorts (dyed RGB value 139,255,99).[2]
