Bamboo Pole
Use in the water to catch fish.
Information
Previous Tier: Training Rod
Next Tier: Fiberglass Rod
Cost: 500g
Sold by: Fish Shop
The Bamboo Pole is a tool used to catch fish. One is obtained from Willy in the cutscene following the completion of the To The Beach quest. Afterwards, it can be purchased from his Fish Shop for 500g. It cannot have bait or tackle attached to it.
