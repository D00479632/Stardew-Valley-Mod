Way Of The Wind pt. 1
You run a little bit faster.
Information
Source Bookseller
Sell Price 5,000g
The Way Of The Wind pt. 1 is a power book that can be purchased from the Bookseller for 15,000g.
The first reading grants the player a power that gives them a permanent +0.25 Speed buff.[1] Once read, it can be found in the Player's Menu on the Special Items & Powers tab. Each subsequent reading gives the player 20 XP in each of the 5 skills.
After reading this book for the first time, the player is able to purchase Way Of The Wind pt. 2 from the Bookseller.
