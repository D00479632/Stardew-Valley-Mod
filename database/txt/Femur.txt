The Femur is a club weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for 350g after reaching floor 10 in The Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on floor 10 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.
History
1.5: Previously unobtainable weapon introduced.[1] Weapon level increased from 1 to 2, sell price from 50 to 100.
1.6.9: Added to Adventurer's Guild shop inventory.
