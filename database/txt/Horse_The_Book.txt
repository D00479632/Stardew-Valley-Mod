Horse: The Book
You gain a little extra speed when riding a horse.
Information
Source Bookseller
Sell Price 1,000g
Horse: The Book is a power book that can be purchased from the Bookseller for 25,000g.
The first reading grants the player a power that makes them gain Speed (+0.5) when riding their horse.[1] Once read, it can be found in the Player's Menu on the Special Items & Powers tab. Each subsequent reading gives the player 20 XP in each of the 5 skills.

Tailoring
Horse: The Book is not used in any tailoring. It can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane. It can also be used as a brown dye when used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed.
