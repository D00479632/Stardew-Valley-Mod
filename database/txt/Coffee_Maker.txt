Coffee Maker
Automatically brews a fresh cup every morning.
Information
Source "Gifts for George" Special Order
Sell Price Cannot be sold
The Coffee Maker is a piece of equipment that automatically brews a single cup of Coffee in the morning. It is received in the mail after completing Evelyn's "Gifts for George" Special Order.
If the original is lost, a replacement can be purchased at the Lost Items Shop in the Secret Woods for 10,000g.

1.5: Introduced.
