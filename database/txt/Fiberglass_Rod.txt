Fiberglass Rod
Use in the water to catch fish.
Information
Previous Tier: Bamboo Pole
Next Tier: Iridium Rod
Cost: 1,800g
Improvements: Ability to use bait
Unlocked at: Fishing Level 2
Sold by: Fish Shop
The Fiberglass Rod is a tool used to catch fish. It can be purchased at Willy's Fish Shop for 1,800g upon reaching level 2 Fishing. It can have bait but not tackle attached to it.
