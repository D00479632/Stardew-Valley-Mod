Warp Totem: Island
Warp directly to Ginger Island. Consumed on use.

The Warp Totem: Island is a Warp Totem that transports the player to Ginger Island by selecting it in inventory and right-clicking. It can be crafted after purchasing the recipe from the Volcano Dwarf for 10,000g. One is obtained after repairing the Farm Obelisk on Ginger Island.

Tailoring
The Warp Totem: Island can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Totem Mask. It can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.
