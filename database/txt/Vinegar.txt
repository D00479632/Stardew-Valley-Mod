Vinegar is a cooking ingredient that can be used in a variety of recipes. It can be purchased for 200g at Pierre's General Store. It can also be purchased from George's shop at the Desert Festival for 5 Calico Eggs. Two Vinegar can also be made by putting Rice into a Keg.
When used on a tree, it prevents the tree from growing Moss.
