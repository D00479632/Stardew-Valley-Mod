The Official Cap is a hat that can be purchased from the Abandoned House for 2,000g after earning the "Ol' Mariner" Achievement (catch 24 different fish). It can also be obtained from Emily's outfit services at the Desert Festival. There is a ≈2% chance[1] to receive the gender-neutral outfit with the Official Cap.

Notes
The Official Cap outfit also includes the Officer Uniform and Farmer Pants (dyed RGB value 53,52,91).[2]
Trivia
This hat may be a reference to the recurring character, Harris, from Harvest Moon. In Harvest Moon 64, he was the town's mailman, and in Harvest Moon: Back to Nature and Friends of Mineral Town, he is the town's policeman.
