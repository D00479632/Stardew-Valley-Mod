The Templar's Blade is a sword weapon that can be purchased for 4,000g from the Adventurer's Guild after reaching floor 55 in The Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on floor 80 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.
History
1.5: Level lowered from 6 to 5 and sell price lowered from 300g to 250g. Bug where weapon defense had no effect fixed.
