The Wood Mallet is a club weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for 2,000g after reaching floor 40 in The Mines. It may also be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on floors 40-59 in The Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on floor 60 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.
History
1.5: Level raised from 2 to 3 and sell price raised from 100g to 150g. Added as a possible reward on floor 60 in the remixed Mines.
