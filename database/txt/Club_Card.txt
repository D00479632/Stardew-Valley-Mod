Club Card
Information
Source "The Mysterious Qi" Quest
Sell Price Cannot be sold
The Club Card is obtained after completing "The Mysterious Qi" Quest. It is used to enter the Casino.
Once obtained, it can be found in the Special Items & Powers tab in The Player's Menu.
